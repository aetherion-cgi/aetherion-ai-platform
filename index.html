Architecture

Built in Layers. Governed at Every Level.

Eleven purpose-built engines forming a coherent intelligence stack — not random features, but a unified architecture for institutional AI.

Engine 01

Universal Intelligence Engine

The universal entry surface. Normalizes intake, shapes requests, attaches context, and initiates orchestration. One canonical interface for all interactions.

Engine 02

Function Broker

The control-plane logic layer. Determines what capabilities are required, routes to the right services, and enforces governance hooks before any action executes.

Engine 03

Cortex Gateway

A hardened mediator between the orchestration plane and domain cognition. Protects sensitive reasoning domains from uncontrolled access.

Engine 04

Domain Cortex

Specialized intelligence for energy, finance, defense, healthcare, logistics, real estate, and more. Each domain operates with its own tools, schemas, and decision thresholds.

Engine 05

Internal Learning Engine

Structured telemetry-based continuous learning. ILE applies credibility tiers to observations, preserving institutional memory and compounding operational performance over time.

Engine 06

UDOA — Data Orchestration

Governed data access across fragmented enterprise environments. Provider abstraction, query planning, and data routing with full traceability and schema awareness.

Engine 07

CEOA — Compute & Energy

Compute and energy-aware routing. Routes workloads based on infrastructure cost, carbon efficiency, latency, sovereignty constraints, and institutional sustainability goals.

Engine 08

Business Underwriting Engine

Quantitative analysis for commercial real estate, enterprise investments, and structured financial scenarios. Monte Carlo analysis, risk-informed recommendation support.

Engine 09

URPE — Risk & Probabilistic

High-consequence risk analysis for governments and critical infrastructure. Multi-variable risk modeling, scenario ranking, and strategic uncertainty evaluation.

Engine 10

Möbius Layer

Meta-coherence engine. Detects contradictions across the intelligence stack, reinforces successful orchestration patterns, and prevents repeated failures from recurring.

Engine 11

Klein Layer

Resource-path optimization layer. Works alongside CEOA and UDOA to make the execution substrate continuously self-optimizing across compute, energy, and data flows.
